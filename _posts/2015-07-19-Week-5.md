### Tip of The Week

Recently, I have taken the time to customize my bash prompt regarding various
tools and color schemes. Although not really necessary to get the job done, it
is nice to have things customized to make coding a more enjoyable experience.
So I am recommending to check out a terminal called
[iTerm2](https://www.iterm2.com/), which is a terminal replacement for Mac OS
X. (Sorry Windows and Linux users). Anyway, it has a lot of great features,
such as various integrations with tmux and etc. You can also tweak color
schemes. Here is a site providing a whole assortment of awesome schemes:
[http://iterm2colorschemes.com/](http://iterm2colorschemes.com/).
